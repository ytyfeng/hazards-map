The Journey to an Easier Life

Steve's daredevil journey of manipulating demonic powers to make them perform data entry for him

Once upon a time, Steve was a lowly junior accountant who had to work overtime to perform tedious data entry.

Feeling miserable and exhausted, he started to ponder if there is a better way...

"Life is too short to enter data manually," he cried.
